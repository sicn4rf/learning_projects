Reviews

Francis' movies

1. Demon Slayer: Infinity Castle 1: 9.9/10

By far my favorite movie of the year. I'm a huge demon slayer fan and this movie did not disappoint. Seeing the manga come to life was unreal, Ufotable did an amazing job with choreographing the fights and turning somewhat bland manga fight scenes into intense and emotional big screen battles. The movie made me cry and the soundtrack was captivating. My only critique is that they didn't announce Zenitsu's win, after he solo'd Upper Moon 6

2. Superman: 8.7/10

I honestly think this might've been one of the best DC live action movies of all time. James Gunn is a unique director and you can see it in his production quality. Not much to say, it's definitely a breath of fresh air from everything we've received from DCCU so far. I will be watching the rest of what James Gunn has prepared for DC

3. Fantastic Four: First Steps: 8.3/10

It was aight. Not the biggest fan of the casting. Looking forward to Spider-Man tho!
